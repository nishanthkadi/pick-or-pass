# Portfolio

This folder is for polished, shareable project outputs.

A finished portfolio entry should include:

- A one-line product description.
- The user problem and target user.
- The AI capability demonstrated.
- Demo link, screenshots, video, or interactive artifact.
- Product decisions and tradeoffs.
- Metrics or evaluation approach.
- Reflection on what you learned and what you would improve.

The audience is someone evaluating your PM judgment, AI fluency, and ability to turn ambiguous ideas into shipped product work.
